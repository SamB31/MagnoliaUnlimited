MAGNOLIA UNLIMITED

GENERAL SERVICES

Magnolia Unlimited offers an array of services to help NCAA athletes reach their maximum potential in the NIL era of athletics. Magnolia Unlimited offers comprehensive services designed to help NCAA athletes and prospective athletes reach their goals.

CONTRACT NEGOTIATION

Magnolia Unlimited has solidified itself in the sports marketplace as one of the premier contract negotiating firms. Our management team uses extensive preparation, research, and planning to generate maximum compensation for our clients.

ADVISORY

Magnolia Unlimited offers its expertise in NIL advising and consulting to current, prospective NCAA athletes and their families on their options no matter what sport they participate in.

MARKETING & BRAND DEVELOPMENT

Magnolia Unlimited will provide comprehensive and individualized marketing goals for current and prospective NCAA athletes and implement a strategic and tailored experience to maximize NIL potential.

COMMUNITY RELATIONS

Magnolia Unlimited will encourage and assist its clients in participating in charitable and socially conscious activities, including foundation development, event and fund raising planning and implementation and other charitable endeavors.

POST-CAREER PLANNING

Magnolia Unlimited is dedicated to athletes when they are on the field and when they retired from the field. We will help athletes set their goals, so that they have a life built after athletics.

FINANCIAL SERVICES

Magnolia Unlimited provides necessary assistance to clients in identifying financial professionals who work with the clients and their families to work towards their individual financial goals.

LEGAL SERVICES

Beginning in 2022 Magnolia Unlimited will have dedicated attorneys to work with your client in all aspects of needed legal services.
